-- MODAL CLOSE BEHAVIOR --

Three ways to close the modal:
  1. The ✕ button in the header
  2. Clicking the overlay backdrop (the outer fixed div), 
     but NOT clicking inside the modal container itself.
     Implement this with an onClick on the outer overlay 
     div and stopPropagation on the inner container div.
  3. Pressing the Escape key.
     Add a useEffect that attaches a keydown listener 
     for "Escape" when isOpen is true and removes it 
     on cleanup.

-- SCROLL LOCK --

Add a useEffect in LegalModal.jsx:
  When isOpen becomes true: 
    document.body.style.overflow = 'hidden'
  When isOpen becomes false or component unmounts: 
    document.body.style.overflow = ''
